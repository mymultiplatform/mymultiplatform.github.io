Answer objections the same way premium ecommerce does.

The Burst footer and support sections rely on compact accordions. This gives you that same clean interaction pattern for launch questions without turning the page into a wall of text.

Did you copy Burst’s assets?

No. This build recreates the page dynamics and structural patterns only: sticky header behavior, sliders, marquees, cards, and accordions. The visuals, code, and placeholders here are original.

Why no real product images yet?

You asked to skip replicating the images. That keeps the prototype safe while still letting us build the shopping experience and refine your own brand direction.

Can this connect to your PayPal page next?

Yes. The next step can be wiring your product CTAs to the hosted PayPal flow or embedding a more polished pre-checkout handoff inside this subpath.

Can we replace the placeholder cards with your real products later?

Absolutely. The layout is already structured for product renders, comparison graphics, videos, and trust badges once you’re ready.
