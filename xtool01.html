Designer – Entry Level

📍 New York, NY (Hybrid) 🧑‍🎓 Full-time · Early career 📅 Posted 2 days ago

The Cannon Corporation is looking for a curious, creative Entry Level Designer to join our multidisciplinary team. You’ll work alongside senior designers, strategists, and technologists to shape digital experiences, brand identities, and visual stories across our portfolio. This is an ideal role for recent graduates or designers with 0–2 years of experience.

  • Collaborate on user-centered design projects from concept to execution.
  • Support the creative team in producing wireframes, mockups, and prototypes.
  • Participate in design critiques and iterate based on feedback.
  • Contribute to brand guidelines, presentations, and marketing assets.

What we look for: proficiency in Figma or Adobe Creative Suite, strong visual sensibility, and a growth mindset. Portfolio or work samples are a plus – show us your potential.

We celebrate diverse perspectives — even if you don't meet every bullet, we encourage you to apply.
