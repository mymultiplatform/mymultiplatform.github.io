Focus Path Blocker Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

Focus Path Blocker is designed to run locally on your Mac. The current release does not require a cloud account and does not transmit browsing history to MyMultiplatform servers by default.

  • URL checks are processed locally for blocking and redirect logic.
  • No ad tracking or third-party analytics are embedded in the blocker runtime.
  • Support emails may include troubleshooting details you choose to share.

If product telemetry is added in future versions, this page will be updated before rollout.

Contact: software@mymultiplatform.com
